What is AI Speak Wizard

"AI Speak wizard is an AI-powered speaking assistant that helps you practice English speaking on the
go and improve your communication skills by recording and AI-analyzing your speech – without a
teacher or a speaking partner. It also makes accurate and almost instant English-level assessment,
which allows businesses to automate respective tasks."

Our mission

By improving language skills, we help people

Although English-speaking skills are a necessity nowadays, learning a language is not easy for
everyone. As non-native speakers, we have struggled for many years to reach a sufficient level of
speaking confidence. This is how we started AI-Speak Wizard - with the belief in mind that there is a
way to create a learning experience that would be accessible anytime and would bring notable
results within just a couple of months.

By improving education, we aim to create inclusive prosperity.
